Reduce & Reuse Guide

Simple ways to minimize waste in your daily life

Kitchen Waste

Biodegradable waste from your kitchen

  • Convert vegetable scraps into compost
  • Use coffee grounds as plant fertilizer
  • Create natural cleaning solutions from citrus peels
  • Start a small herb garden using kitchen scraps

Household Items

Non-biodegradable everyday items

  • Transform old clothes into cleaning rags
  • Repurpose glass jars as storage containers
  • Use cardboard boxes for organization
  • Create decorative items from plastic bottles

Electronic Waste

Separated electronic materials

  • Donate working electronics to schools
  • Trade-in old devices for newer models
  • Recycle your old phones at certified collection points
  • Keep devices for e-waste recycling events
